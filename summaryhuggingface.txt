 Ananda College is a prestigious Buddhist school in Maradana, Colombo . It was established as the English Buddhist School by Colonel Henry Steel Olcott in 1886 . It provides primary and secondary education on a campus of 15 acres (61,000Â m2)  The Ananda College war memorial is situated in front of the Henry Steel Olcott Hall and is dedicated to alumni of Ananda college who died while members of the Sri Lankan armed forces . Ananda Daham Pasala (Ananda Dhamma School) is the Sunday school . The Olcott oration is an annual event .
